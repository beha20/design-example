<p>Clothing Line
Early 2020 he announced on his social media page that he will be releasing his clothing brand BlackGenes.us this spring. It’s a full inventory of sweaters and sweats. Soon to be a shoe line associated with the brand by spring 2021. The nonprofit is meant to help cultured family around the us who can’t afford clothing or back to school supplies. With the artist background being one which he can relate to, he opened up this brand to shed light on his values.
<a href="#">www.BlackGenes.Us</a> and <a href="https://www.stajeofficial.com/">www.stajeofficial.com </a></p>

<p>StaJe co-partnered with his longtime manager Andy Jin and label mate Hamilton to create their record Fam Entertainment. “We believe that every artist should have a family and a team they can trust and believe in” StaJe says in a recent article with yahoo news. He currently has 3 Los Angeles based artist signed to the label and is working with collaborating with major labels to help kick start his artist development program.
www.Fam-Entertainment.net</p>
